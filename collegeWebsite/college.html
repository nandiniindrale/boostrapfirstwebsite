SHARADA INSTITUTE PUNE

Hostel

The hostel was started in 2006, after SHARADA INSTITUE was established in 2004. The Institute has two buildings for boys hostel to accommodate 600 students and three buildings for girls hostel to accommodate 618 girls.

Ground

On sports front the institute provides indoor facilities such as air conditioned well equipped Gymnasium, Table tennis hall. Outdoor sports spacious arena include volley ball court, basket ball court, cricket turf wicket ground, 400 m athletic track, tennis clay court with flood lights and badminton court.We have very good sports facilities! We also have a permanent faculty looking after Sports Department.

Library

The college has a well-stocked Library with over 12953 books, 13 periodicals and subscribes to a no of e-journals and 05 newspapers. It also has a wide range of reference books and encyclopedias to cater to the needs of the students and the staff members. The Reference Section is also regularly upgraded. The library is also being automated.
